About Omar

Hi There! My name is Umar (Omar) Shahbaz, welcome to my Portfolio Website. A little about myself, I am a 20 year old Saudi student, who is doing IT with a major in Tech Management. I have been doing IT as early as the 6th Grade, and when I was choosing a degree to purse, I knew, regardless of what field it is, it had to involve IT.

I'd say playing my guitar in my spare time, or any time of the day to be honest, is my go-to hobby. I am always looking for more ways to improve my hand on the guitar, since it has been less than a year since I started playing
